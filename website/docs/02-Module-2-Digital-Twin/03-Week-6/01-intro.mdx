# Gazebo Physics & Sensors

## Inertia and Mass

For physics to work, every link needs an `<inertial>` tag.
- **Mass**: In kg.
- **Inertia Matrix**: ixx, iyy, izz.

## Adding Sensors

We attach "plugins" to our URDF to simulate sensors.

### Lidar Plugin
```xml
<plugin name="laser_controller" filename="libgazebo_ros_ray_sensor.so">
  <ros>
    <argument>~/out:=scan</argument>
  </ros>
  <output_type>sensor_msgs/LaserScan</output_type>
</plugin>
```
